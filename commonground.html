Common Ground

Common Ground Cindy Nord Center for Renewal is a non-profit located in the beautiful Vermilion River Valley near Oberlin, Ohio. Common Ground focuses on improving lives through conscious living activities. Some activities include a summer camp for children and teens, an annual fire walk, and various workshops for all ages that focus on finding the true potential within yourself.

In 2014, to help support the mission of Common Ground, Common Ground Canopy Tours opened. The canopy tour is a two and a half hour long tour in the treetops through the Vermilion River Valley that takes you across 7 zip lines, up 2 spiral staircases, across 3 arial bridges and a floating staircase, all leading to a final rappel at the end.

Common Ground Canopy Tours needed a map that would illustrate all of the features of the course and capture the fun, inviting attitude of Common Ground, yet be accurate to the geography. I worked with them to illustrate something that could be used for print and on the web. The illustration was first done digitally, then gone over manually with chalk and colored pencil and scanned for reproduction.

Kent State Softball

In my time at Kent State Athletics Marketing, I have been lucky enough to work on many unique projects. A highlight from last year was having the chance to design the Kent State Softball stadium graphics.Overall the project took about a month and a half to complete. Throughout the design process I consulted members within the organization to create something that rivals and exceeds what other schools are doing. The final iteration of the design features the elements of the current brand while making a statement of power.

Universal Windows Direct

Universal Windowes Direct is a national home improvement company started in 2002 in Cleveland, Ohio. As a digital designer at UWD I help create a digital presence that customers connect with online. This includes designing landing pages, social media graphics and much more.

Landing Page: After Redesign

When redesigning the landing page I wanted to create a more streamlined form. Due to the short attention span of users, the form is the most important section and commands the hierarchy. By making the form appear shorter, it appears less overwhelming to users. Other improvements included cutting content and allowing more opportunities to gain trust.

The redesign was quite successful, taking the average conversion rate from a 5.5% conversion rate to a 6.5% conversion rate resulting in thousands more conversions and consultations booked. That's over 2% higher than the remodeling industry average (remodeling.hw.net).

Before Redesign

Before the redesign, aside from being visually unpleasing, it wasn't performing as it should. The form was cumbersome, there was too much information, the sections had low contrast and it looked outdated.

Iconography

I like to highlight some of the fine details like our iconography. I've played a role in developing a new set of icons and patterns to be used on social media and website pages. These small but mighty illustrative elements help clarify information and can create patterns that add texture.

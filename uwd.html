Universal Windows Direct

Universal Windowes Direct is a national home improvement company started in 2002 in Cleveland, Ohio. As a digital designer at UWD I help create a digital presence that customers connect with online. This includes designing landing pages, social media graphics and much more.

Iconography

I like to highlight some of the fine details like our iconography. I've played a role in developing a new set of icons and patterns to be used on social media and website pages. Thes small but mighty illustrative elements help clarify information and can create patterns that add texture.

Landing Page

When redesigning the landing page I wanted to place an emphasis on simplicity. Due to the short attention span of users, the form is the most important section and commands the hierarchy. Following that are opportunities for users to learn more about our company and products broken up in different sections to make information more digestable and interesting.

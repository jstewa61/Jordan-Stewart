Millennial audiences are not as engaged in books as they once were. There are many timeless, classic books that need rebranding to captivate younger audiences. I chose to re-design S.E. Hinton’s The Outsiders and Elie Wiesel’s Night.

One of my favorite covers to design was Elie Wiesel’s Night. Elie was a Holocaust surviver and he tells his story of the horrors he witnessed. It was a big task to create something that would be respectful to the content. The cover features photography of hand woven barbed wire typography. The back features a famous quote by Elie in black ink on black paper which can be seen in the light.

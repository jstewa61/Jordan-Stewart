Night Book Cover

Elie was a Holocaust surviver and he tells his story of the horrors he witnessed. It was a big task to create something that would be respectful towards the content. I designed the cover to give a hint towards the content through the hand woven barbed wire. The back features an uplifting quote by Elie in black ink on black paper which can be seen in the light.

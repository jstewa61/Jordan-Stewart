Travel Illustration

One of my favorite parts about illustration is the different techniques you can apply to convey your message. In this travel illustration displaying Cedar Point, my goal was to create something that would express the feeling of being at the amusement park. I experimented a lot with different techniques and ended up using Adobe Illustrator overlaid with chalk marker and colored pencil.

Cat's Cradle

In the work below, I was asked to create a movie title for the book Cat's Cradle by Kurt Vonnegut. Cat's Cradle is a story of how mankind can destroy itself with its own irresponsibility. In the titles I sought to create the feeling of a World War II dystopian society by making a black and white movie and using government archived video footage and audio alongside footage I filmed. Like the book's title, the sequence revolves around the cat's cradle, a symbol used throughout the book.

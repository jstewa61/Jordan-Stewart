REI Annual Report

REI is an outdoor equipment retailer based in Kent, Washington. In this annual report REI wanted to show their numbers for the year while telling a narrative. REI is not only an outdoor goods retailer, but a source of social good too. In this annual report, the narrative is their new Force of Nature campaign aimed at getting more women outdoors.

I wanted to showcase the playfulness of the brand through the sketchy elements shown throughout. The report was printed on completely recycled paper to support REI’s environmentally conscious efforts. I chose to stitch bind the report together to continue the organic feeling of the pages.

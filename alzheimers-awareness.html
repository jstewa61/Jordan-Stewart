Alzheimers Awareness Interactive Campaign

Alzheimer's Disease is a growing public health concern as it becomes more common among the aging population. The goal of this campaign was to target young people to donate to the cause. To do this, my aim was to create a series of video ads and a micro-site to act as a landing page to learn more and donate.

Pangea: The Puzzle

Continental drift is a very difficult topic to grasp for some students. Pangea: The Puzzle is a board game and app design concept which explains continental drift to students in a fun and interactive way.

The pieces are simplified and connect perfectly to show the concept more easily. The icons on the edges of the pieces help to direct the students to put it together properly and are later used to explain how fossils helped the researchers discover continental drift.

Pangea: The Puzzle encompassed two parts–the physical board game as well as the interactive mobile app. Users first learn about the processes of how continental drift took place though the dialogue and motion graphics. Next, the users can use the fossil finder which opens the camera of the mobile device. When the camera is pointed at certain marked locations on the board, a fact about that fossil appears and explains how it helped researchers in theorizing continental drift.

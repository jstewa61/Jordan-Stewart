Pangea: The Puzzle

Continental drift is a very difficult topic to grasp for some students. Pangea: The Puzzle is a board game and interactive app design concept which explains continental drift to students in an exciting and interactive way. The video below shows the concept in action.

Brand Elements

My goal was to be very playful with the colors and typography to make it interesting for the students and to differentiate the information. I decided to use a lot of bright colors, but included brown in the typography and a gritty texture to bring in an earthy feel.

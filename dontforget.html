Alzheimer's Awareness Poster

Almost 5.7 million people are suffering from Alzheimer's Disease. Although the disease is so prominent, it is one of the most underfunded compared to other common health conditions. Since Alzheimer's has affected my family and I personally, I sought to create a poster that illustrated the facts for people who may not know about the disease.

I was inspired by vintage medical diagrams which shows through the line work, shading technique and typography treatment. The torn paper represents the mind being taken away from those who suffer from the disease.

Kent State Football Season Tickets

One of the most most important aspects of the gameday experience is a fan's ticket into the game. A ticket is not only a way into the game, but a keepsake to remember the game. Since fans often times hold on to their tickets, it is important to make visually be something they can be proud to display.
Kent State Football needed a striking design for the 2018 football season tickets. I was given the opportunity to work with our ticketing department to design tickets that would be a companion to the new "FlashFAST campaign".
